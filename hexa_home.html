Banking and Financial Services

Helping customers embark on a transformative digitalization journey

The banking and financial services industry operates in the ‘new normal’ today, dominated by ever-tightening regulations, digital banking, and omni-channel customer experiences. Compressing costs, including high Run-the-Bank (RTB) costs, due to the large FTE based pricing models, is a major challenge for the industry. Succeeding in this environment requires industry players to strike a balance between operational optimization and digital transformation. While technologies such as Big Data, cloud, mobile, and IoT are already transforming the way business is done, banks need to optimize data management. Leveraging analytics will help them boost enterprise resilience, increase cross-selling opportunities, enhance customer value, and maximize their wallet share.

What we offer

Our rich experience in partnering with leading global banks and financial institutions enables us to offer comprehensive solutions that meet the varying evolving needs of the banking industry. Our analytics and data management solutions delivered through a process driven approach help banks successfully embark on their ‘data to digital’ journey to achieve the desired end-to-end transformation of their operations.
